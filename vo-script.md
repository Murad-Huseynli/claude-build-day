## How to record (Mac, free)
1. Open **QuickTime Player → File → New Audio Recording** (or the **Voice Memos** app). Quiet room, mic ~20 cm away, no fan/AC.
2. Open `worldline-demo-final.mp4` in another window (muted). Hit **record**, then **play the video** and read each line as its caption appears.
3. One clean take is enough. Stop, then **export/save** the audio (m4a, mp3, or wav) — e.g. to `~/Desktop/worldline-vo.m4a`.
4. Tell me the file path. I'll **mux it onto the video and tune the sync** (`ffmpeg` map video+audio), and hand back `worldline-demo-voiced.mp4`.

**Tip:** glance at the captions as your teleprompter — they're timed to the lines, so following them keeps you in sync automatically.
